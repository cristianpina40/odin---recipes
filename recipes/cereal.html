Cereal is a very common food round the world that comes in a huge amount of varieties

In this tutorial we will be showing you how to make cereal.

Ingredients

  • A bowl or cup
  • A spoon
  • Cereal of your choice
  • Milk of any kind
  • An appetite ;|

Steps

  1. Get Your Supplies - Buy all the supplies listed above.
  2. Grab your bowl or cup and pour your desired amount of cereal into the bowl.
  3. Pour your choice of milk into the bowl.
  4. MAKE SURE THAT YOU DO STEPS 2 BEFORE YOU DO STEP 3, UNLESS YOU'RE AN ANIMAL!
  5. Grab your spoon and enjoy.
